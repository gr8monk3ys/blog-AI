Startups need leverage. AI gives small teams a way to move fast without sacrificing quality. The key is to treat AI as an assistant for research, structure, and iteration while keeping editorial ownership in the hands of humans.

In this guide, we outline a simple content workflow: research prompts, outline generation, draft expansion, and human editing. It is designed to scale without losing brand voice.
